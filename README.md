## 🛠 Setting Up the Bot

Follow these steps to prepare the software for your first trade:

1. Open the application after the installation finishes.
2. A window will appear in your default web browser for the setup process.
3. Select your broker from the provided list.
4. Input your brokerage account credentials into the secure fields. The bot uses these to log into your account and place orders based on your rules.
5. Set your hard money limits. This is a safety feature that prevents the bot from spending more than the amount you specify.
6. Choose the stocks or ETFs you want to trade.

Verify that all credentials match your brokerage account details. The system encrypts these details locally on your machine to keep them safe.

## 📊 Backtesting Your Strategy

Before trading with real money, you should test your rules against past market data. This process is called backtesting. It shows you how a specific strategy would have performed over the last year.

1. Navigate to the Backtest tab in the web interface.
2. Select the timeframe you wish to test.
3. Choose the technical indicators you want to monitor, such as moving averages or signal strength.
4. Click the Run Test button.
5. Review the generated graphs and reports.

If the results fit your goals, you can proceed to activate the live bot.

## 🤖 Running the Trading Bot

Once you configure your settings and test your strategy, you can turn on the live trading mode.

1. Go to the Dashboard tab.
2. Ensure your internet connection stays active.
3. Toggle the switch to Live Trading.
4. The bot will now monitor live market data streams.
5. It will execute trades automatically when your preset conditions are met.

Keep the application open while the market is active. You can minimize the window to your taskbar; it will continue to run in the background.

## 🛡 Staying Safe

Trading stocks carries financial risk. This tool performs actions automatically, which can lead to unexpected losses if your strategy is faulty. Always start with small amounts of money to ensure your settings work as intended. Monitor your account regularly through your broker’s website to verify the trades. You have full control, and you can stop the bot at any time by pressing the Stop button in the interface.

## ⚙️ Advanced Settings

The software includes a parameter optimizer for power users. This feature helps you find the most effective settings for your chosen stocks by running thousands of variations of your strategy. Use this if you want to improve your trade consistency. The web interface logs all activities, allowing you to review past actions if you need to troubleshoot a specific trade or investigate a market movement.

## ❓ Troubleshooting

If the bot fails to connect, check your internet connection first. Ensure that your brokerage account is active and that your API keys are correct. If the application closes unexpectedly, restart the program. Most issues resolve with a simple restart. Should you experience ongoing problems, check the log file located in the folder where you installed the program.
